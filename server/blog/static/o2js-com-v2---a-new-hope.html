o2js.com v2 - A New Hope

By the end of this month (Aug 30, 2013) I will be shutting down the old o2.js blog, and rewriting the articles one by one to this blog. I have several reasons for that:

1. I want to re-organize the articles into more meaningful categories;
2. Since the technology evolves faster than my writing speed, some of these articles need serious editing and rewriting.
3. While writing my book I've learned a lot about how to present information – knowing something, and expressing your knowledge to a broader audience are two different things. So an overall editing process would be useful.

And I thought the best way to make it happen is
to start with a blank slate ;).

Since I have backups of the former blog's database, I haven't lost anything.

I will also be shooting multiple birds with one stone, since one of my goals is to convert all my blog posts to markdown.
