Hack the World Like You Never Did Before

This February, the 7th #hack4good will be in 50 cities, with 5,000+ developers and NGOs in tandem, worldwide.

Hack4… What?

Yes! #hack4Good 0.5 in a city Near you, this February (2014) 7th to 9th!

You can hack online too if you’re not attending in person.

#hack4good is a hackathon for the good of the community; you may want to look at the former hacks done to get a feeling about it.

When you attend the hackathon, you will be hacking on projects from great minds like yours and those of the respected NGOs, and non-profits including

  • Amnesty International,
  • Oxfam,
  • Global Fund,
  • GOAL Ireland,
  • UN OCHA,
  • NPR Labs,
  • and many more… all contributing ideas to help hack a better world.

Everyone Is Welcome; Everyone Is Invited, Everyone Is Involved

Registration is open, and totally free.

The event is being joined by some of the world’s most renowned technologists including…

  • The builder of the iPhone;
  • The Director of Global Products for Walmart.com;
  • The creator of BitTorrent… and more.

I Will Be There ;)
